Roasted Beef Recipe

Description

This roasted beef is the ultimate comfort foodâ€”juicy, perfectly pink in the center, and wrapped in a golden herb crust. We start with a well-marbled cut, rub it down with garlic, thyme, and olive oil, then roast it low and slow until it melts in your mouth. Pair it with roasted carrots and a splash of gravy for a cozy, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup beef broth or red wine (for the pan)
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh rosemary and thyme (for aromatics)
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 carrots, cut into chunks (optional, for roasting with the beef)

Steps

  1. Let the beef come to room temperature
  2. Preheat your oven
  3. Season the beef
  4. Make the herb crust
  5. Prep your roasting pan
  6. Roast (high heat sear)
  7. Lower temp and roast to finish
  8. Rest the roast
  9. Slice & serve
